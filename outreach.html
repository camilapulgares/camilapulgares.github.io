Outreach

I am passionate about communicating astronomy to the public. During my bachelor and master I participaed actively in the Antü-Küyen outreach group, an astronomy club at Universidad de Chile. I also had the opportunity to give several talks in different regions of Chile by being part of the Centro de Astrofísica y Tecnologías Afines (CATA).
